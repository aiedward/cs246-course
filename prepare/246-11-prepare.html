Team Activity Preparation - Javadocs

Before meeting with your team for your team activity, please go through the following tutorials regarding Javadocs:

  • http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Javadoc
  • http://code.tutsplus.com/tutorials/learn-java-for-android-development-javadoc-code-documentation--mobile-3674

Keep in mind that the previous link discusses using Javadocs in Eclipse. To generate them in Android Studio, you can click "Tools -> Generate JavaDoc..." and then select the appropriate options and specify an output directory.

Then re-read the Javadoc section from our style guide:

  • https://google-styleguide.googlecode.com/svn/trunk/javaguide.html#s7-javadoc
