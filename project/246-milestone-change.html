Milestone Change Request

If circumstances require you to modify the deliverables for a milestone, please prepare a short document with the following sections and email it to the instructor. The instructor will then grant or deny the change request.

Please note two critical items regarding change requests:

  1. Change requests cannot be submitted after Monday night on the week the milestone is due.
  2. The reason for a change request should be major unforeseen circumstances or challenges, and NOT “we haven'’t been able to devote as much time as we hoped.”
  3. To submit your request, download this PDF form and email it to your instructor.
