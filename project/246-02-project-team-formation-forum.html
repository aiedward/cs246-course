Team Formation Forum

As noted in the Team Formation instructions, you need to self-identify teams of 3 these week. This forum is designed to aid in that process.

In this forum, please take a minute to introduce yourself. Tell us each a little about yourself and where you come from. Then, please list the project ideas that seem most interesting to you at this point.

Then, please comment on other people's posts to start to come together on a team and potential ideas. You don't need to commit to an idea this week, but hopefully you have a direction in mind.

There are no points associated with this forum, but rather, the assignment is to submit your team recommendation. This is simply a help toward that end.
