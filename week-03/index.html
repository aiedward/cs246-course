Software Design and Development :: CS 246

Week 03 :: Overview

Summary

This week you will learn about Java Interfaces.

Make sure you complete the following:

  1. 03 Prepare : Reading

    To prepare yourself for the assignments this week, read the information found in the 03 Prepare Reading assignment, then complete the accompanying quiz on I-Learn.

  2. 03 Teach : Team Activity

    Face-to-Face students will complete the Teach assignment and quiz in class on Wednesday. Online students should coordinate a time to meet with their teams to complete the activity.

  3. 03 Prove

    After completing the assignment listed in I-Learn, make sure you upload your code to github and take the quiz in I-Learn.

  4. 03 Ponder

    After completing the 03 Prove assignment quiz, the teacher's solution will become available on I-Learn. Compare your solution to that version, then complete the 03 Ponder Dropbox assignment using the provided template.

  5. 03 Team : Team Agreement

    Work with your team to complete the Team Agreement as discussed on I-Learn.
