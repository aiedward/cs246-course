Lesson 13 Overview

This is the final week to get your project wrapped up.

In addition, we your team activity will give you some more practice identifying and applying design patterns. This is good preparation for your final exam next week, which will contain a few design pattern scenarios.

Lesson Objectives:

  1. Be able to recognize and apply design patterns appropriately.
  2. Complete the final project.

Individual Preparation

You will want to complete the reading and individual design pattern quiz before your team activity, so that you have this week's pattern in your toolbelt.

Team Project

This week you need to finish your project and submit it to I-Learn. Then, next week, you will demo / deliver it to your sponsor, and prepare a document that gives your evaluation of the project, but the development needs to be finished this week and a corresponding APK of your project submitted.

Don't forget that each person needs to turn in a weekly status report.
