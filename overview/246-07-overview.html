Lesson 07 Overview

Introduction

This week, the main objective is for your team to complete your project design. Along with the design, you also need to decide on milestone deliverables to serve as checkpoints to your final project deliverable.

In addition, this week your Team Activity will help you set up your team project and commit it to GitHub. It will also help ensure that everyone on the team can commit to the repository and handle merge conflicts.

LESSON OBJECTIVES:

  1. Develop a detailed design for the team project, so you can begin development in earnest
  2. Create a team version control repository
  3. Better understand merge conflicts/resolution in Git

Individual Preparation

The only individual assignment this week is to complete the reading and take the accompanying quizzes. The rest of the time that you would have spent on an individual programming assignment should be devoted to working on your team project design.

Remember that with our focus this week shifting completely to the team project, you are expected to work at least 10 hours every week on the course. The weekly status report will ask you to report the number of hours spent.

Team Project

Make sure to meet with your team early on in the week, so you can work on high level concepts and make assignments for each team member for the week. Then you can meet back up at the end of the week to bring everything together.
