Lesson 08 Overview

The main task for this week is to begin in earnest (if you haven't already) the development of your team project. You should plan to spend the vast majority of your time on that project.

The weekly Team Activity will give you some basic experience with unit tests and the idea of Test Driven Development. Incorporating unit tests into your project will be very beneficial to tracking down bugs later in the semester.

Lesson Objectives:

  1. Understand the principles of unit testing and Test Driven Development.
  2. Make progress toward final team project.

Individual Preparation

The only individual assignment this week is to complete the design pattern reading and take the accompanying quiz. In addition, there is a small amount of preparation material that you should go through before your team meeting to make your time spent together as efficient as possible.

The rest of the time that you would have spent on an individual programming assignment should be devoted to working on your team project.

Team Project

As noted, this is where you will spend the bulk of your time this week (and from here on out). Make sure to observe all items in your team contract, communicate well with each other, and commit your code often. You may find it beneficial to do "pair-programming" with each other for some periods of time, rather than just dividing up and going your separate ways completely.

There are technically no deliverables this week for the project, but you will need to be prepared for your milestone next week.

Don't forget that each person needs to turn in a weekly status report.
