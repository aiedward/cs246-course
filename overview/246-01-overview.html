Lesson 01 Overview

This week we will dive into basic Java concepts and have an assignment that will help you get your feet wet in Java Development. This assignment will require you to follow the basics of what you learn in Head First Java to create and compile a Java program, but will also require you to do some digging on the Internet (finding solutions outside the reading).

Having to find solutions on the Internet, outside your reading, is by design. This is the way much of this course will go, and more importantly, this is the way you will develop software in the real world.

In addition, in order to get a start on the semester projects, you will need to read over the Team Project Overview and give a “pitch” of some first thoughts for ideas for a project. In the next lesson, we will share more ideas and form teams around common interests.
