Team Activity - Android Hello World

Instructions

Your team assignment for this week is to help everyone in your team get a "Hello World" application to compile and run in an Android emulator.

On the surface (and if all goes well), this should be very simple and straightforward, and yet, there always seem to be challenges that arise.

Before meeting as a team, make sure you have each downloaded and installed your IDEs.

This semester we will be using Android Studio for our development environment. This is a powerful IDE that can enable us to do great things, but it does take a little bit of setting up and getting used to.

Completion

Your score for this assignment is as follows:

50% - Getting your own Hello World application to compile and run.

25% - Teammate #1 gets their application to compile and run.

25% - Teammate #2 gets their application to compile and run.

Please mark in the I-Learn assessment for what you were each able to complete by 11:59 PM on Saturday.

If you have two members on your team, count your other teammate as both "Teammate #1" and "Teammate #2". If there are four members on the team, mark "Teammate #2" successful when everyone on the team completes the assignment.
