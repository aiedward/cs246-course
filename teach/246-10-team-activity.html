Team Activity: Logging

Overview

The purpose of this activity is to become more familiar with logging in Android. Make sure to read over the preparation material for this activity before starting.

Instructions

Work together as a team to help each individual do the following:
  1. Insert a log for an error message in at least one place. (Try to put this where an actual error occurs) 
  2. Insert a log for in info message in at least two places.
  3. Ensure that you can see all of your messages in the Log output. (You may need to force a condition in some way to see your error message)
  4. Change the Log display filters to only see the log messages that are Warnings or higher. Then verify that you no longer see the info messages.
  5. Change the Log display filters see all log messages for your particular tag (i.e., in your specific activity). Verify that you do not see any other messages.
